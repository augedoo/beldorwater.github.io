OUR STORY

There was this woman who noticed that the demand for water has increased in her society and always there was shortage. She took it upone herself to sell water around but due to her age she became weak an began to sell the water in a little store in front of her house. Gradually she was able to gather some money to extend the shop and she began to sell the water in bulks. And that was how BELDOR速 Mineral Water came about.
